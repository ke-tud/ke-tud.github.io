Program

The workshop takes place on Sunday, May 26th, in the afternoon.

14:00 – 15:30

  • Invited talk by Luc de Raedt:
    Statistical Relational Learning using ProbLog
  • Antoine Zimmermann, Christophe Gravier, Julien Subercaze, and Quentin Cruzille:
    Nell2RDF: Read the Web, and turn it into RDF (short)

Coffee Break

16:00 – 17:30

  • Isabelle Augenstein, Anna Lisa Gentile, Barry Norton, Ziqi Zhang, and Fabio Ciravegna:
    Mapping Keywords to Linked Data Resources for Automatic Query Expansion (long)
  • Fedelucio Narducci, Matteo Palmonari, and Giovanni Semeraro
    Cross-language semantic matching for discovering links to e-gov services in the LOD cloud (long)
  • Ian Horrocks, Thomas Hubauer, Ernesto Jimenez-Ruiz, Evgeny Kharlamov, Manolis Koubarakis, Ralf Möller, Konstantina Bereta, Christian Neuenstadt, Özgür Özçep, Mikhail Roshchin, Panayiotis Smeros, Dmitriy Zheleznyakov:
    Addressing Streaming and Historical Data in OBDA Systems: Optique’s Approach (short)
  • Jindřich Mynarz and Vojtěch Svátek:
    Towards a Benchmark for LOD-Enhanced Knowledge Discovery from Structured Data (short)
